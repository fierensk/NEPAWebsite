Site Rules

Event site policies for EMR Event Park

Park Rules

  • Smoking in the parking area only
  • No pets (If there is an instance of a service animal please contact us beforehand at nepagm@gmail.com)
  • No underage drinking
  • No fireworks
  • The campsite(s) and cabins must be left in the same condition as they were found:
    1. Campsite must be cleaned of all trash before leaving.
    2. Any campfire wood that is left must be stacked neatly beside the fire ring.
    3. No glass, metal, or garbage must be left in the fire ring.
    4. No destruction, defacing, or moving of EMR property.
  • Fires must be totally contained in a fire ring
