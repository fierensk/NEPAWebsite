Registration

Three steps. One event.

Step One

Register your Character

CMA FREEPLAY

Register your character on the CMA for our event.

Click the CMA button → Log In → Events → Select the event → Start Preregistration

If your character is above the current build cap

Register on both the Freeplay CMA and regular CMA.

  • On the Freeplay, create a character with the current cap’s amount of XP, and build them only with skills you currently have on your main character (but fewer, I assume).
  • Speedrun the rest of the Freeplay CMA preregistration. Only indicate: magic, high magic, and crafting ability memorization lists. No crafting batch or magic item declarations needed there—only in the main CMA.
  • You don’t need to fill in other preregistration questions about food or lodging on Freeplay. The main CMA tracks event credit and logistics; Freeplay is for your character sheet.
If you wish to NPC

Preregister on the main CMA for the desired event, indicating which character you wish to blanket.

If you are brand new to the game, create a blank character with any name/race/class you wish that can hold the blankets you receive as a reward (all of these attributes can be changed by Logistics before you play this character for the first time).

If you have questions about building a character or rules clarifications, hop on down to our Discord and we’ll have your answers.

Step Two

Pay for the event

STORE PAYPAL

Purchase event registration. To feed, house, and entertain you!

You can either purchase an event registration in our store (or one of the shiny, limited stock Event Registration + NPC Buyouts) or send the appropriate amount to us directly via PayPal.

If you are NPCing

Coming to NPC for us for the whole weekend costs you nothing— it's on us. See you there!

Step Three

Prepare yourself

HERITAGES PACK LIST

Gather your costume and camping materials! If this is your first time, check out our recommended packing list over in the New Player section.

Here at Alliance, we have some basic costume requirements. Grab a tunic, some medieval pants, and good footwear. Also check if your character’s heritage has costume or makeup requirements.

If you’re NPCing with us, bring a fully black outfit (no logos please) to run around in and act as a base for costumes we provide. Happy packing!
